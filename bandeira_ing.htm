...In the wind
 
Its worth more than anything,
The name, the face, the image,
After all, the aura that composes an organism or an organization.
 
If CEPEN has a physical face , this face is its flag.
Like a son that heritages genes from his parents,
CEPEN has received the colors from the country it was born.
 
Major synthesis of its existence. Shows its purpose.
Reporting the great universal law of nature, its tendency to equilibrium,
a triangle  with its  base overturned, Neotropical America,
inserted in the green pavilion of life, stage of organic events.
 
The triangle of arrows in action, searches the equilibrium,
harvesting and throwing from and to all sides.
 
This is the CEPENs moment, its best epitome, divided in two phases:
Harvesting and sending resources, harvesting and disseminating knowledge.
 
Link among parallel essences of impossible dissociation.
One, Homo Sapiens oeconomicus, gold-yellow  arrows, praising, sheltering
and opening passages to the other, the Homo Sapiens scientificus,
blue arrows, core fragile and strong, that points to that one the way to follow.
 
One helping the other.
 
Both, products from the dominant species, animal of brilliant talents,
brain exponents prepared and forged on the evolution in a hard way,
but demonstrating vitality, grace, and claw.
Aware, scientist, makes  public:
animal body, brain capable of dreaming, conceiving and realizing,
learning, teaching and loving.
 
   This, to CEPEN , is the portrait of the man, without anthropocentrism, the
most fantastic species ever known.
 
To produce resources and knowledge and disseminate to all winds.
 
Science, art and spirit, sense and emotion, together in action,
searching for a better world.
 
This is CEPEN.
Thats how it was conceived,
this is the way it will be maintained ...and in the wind.
 
 
... and here we go,
 searching  happiness in our little boat called earth,
sailing to drift, in the cosmic space...
 
   .                                                      - Goimbè -
